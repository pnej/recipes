Ingredients:
 - 1 cup macaroni 
 - 1 lb ground been
 - 1/2 cup chopped onion
 - 4 oz can of mushroom slices
 - 14.5 oz can of stewed tomatoes
 - 1 cup chopped celery
 - Boiling water

Directions:
 1. Pour boiling water over macaroni, let sit. 
 
 2. Brown ground beef with onion, drain.  Add the rest of the ingredients 
     including macaroni and until heated through, about 30-45 min.
